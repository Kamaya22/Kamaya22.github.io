Our Solar System

A solar system refers to a star with objects traveling in orbit around it.Ours consist of the sun and eight planets (with their satellites), dwarf planets, asteroids etc... Our solar system is situated inside the Milky Way.

This project was powered by Three.js and WebGL. This scene contains the sun and our eight planets with their orbits. It is not made to scale in order to see the planets in a better way.

For a better viewing experience, use a laptop or desktop with the latest version of Google Chrome or Safari Technology Preview.
